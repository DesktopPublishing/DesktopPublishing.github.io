About us

Imagine a group of seemingly different individuals with the most creative minds gather and team up for one major project. What do we get? Voila, disenyo Magazine! A superb art magazine designed, written and published by the combined efforts of amateur artists and writers. Living up to its name, disenyo ( a literal tagalog translation of design), will surely make its readers delve deep into the world of art and design, making every page worth your time.
